Is Chicken Pastil in Gensan delicious?

November 5th 2024 8 min read

While Binalot is a famous meal in Luzon, Mindanoa has a delicacy called Pastilthat is unique to the region. Chicken Pastil, a traditional Filipino breakfast meal in Mindanao, is made with steamed rice and topped with shredded chicken. A cheap food that may be foun throught Central Mindanao is pasta.

A classic in the Mindanao region, pastil(also called patil,patel,patir, or pater im Maranao, and paster in Iranum) is a dish. Another name for it is "kagikit", a straightforward dish madde with shredded beef,chicken, or fish. The meat is cooked browning it in vegetable oil while sauteing it with onion,garlic, and soy sauce.

To counteract the saltness of the meat, pstil is typically served with pickled vegetable, such as cucumber or mung beans. To complete the dish, you can also add hard-boiled or fried eggs. In order to aid in taste absoption, banana leaves are also wrapped around the meat and rice.

This recipe for the chicken pastil is a great choice for an afternoon ack.

Ingredients:

1/2 cup Canola Oil

2 cloves Garlic minced

350g Chicken boiled & shredded

4 tbsp Soy Sauce

3 tbsp White Vinegar

1 1/2 tbsp White/Brown Sugar

1/2 tsp black pepper

1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder (optional)

2 tbsp Margarine (optional)
